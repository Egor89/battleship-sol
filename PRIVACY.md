# Privacy Policy for Battleship Sol

**Last updated:** March 5, 2026

## Overview

Battleship Sol ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Battleship Sol is designed with privacy in mind:
- ✅ No user accounts or registration required
- ✅ No analytics or tracking
- ✅ No cookies or persistent identifiers
- ✅ No access to device contacts, camera, microphone, or location
- ✅ No data stored on our servers (we don't have any servers)

## Wallet Connection

Battleship Sol uses **Mobile Wallet Adapter (MWA)** to connect to your Solana wallet:

- Your wallet credentials remain entirely under your control
- We never have access to your private keys
- Wallet interactions occur directly between the app and your wallet app
- All transactions require your explicit approval

The app requests your wallet's public address only to:
- Display your SOL balance
- Check for Genesis Token ownership (for exclusive features)
- Submit optional donations (0.001 SOL) to support development

## On-Chain Data

Victory records are stored on the **Solana blockchain** using the Memo Program:

- Victory data is **public** and **permanent** (blockchain nature)
- Contains: timestamp, difficulty level, Genesis Token status
- Does **not** contain personally identifiable information
- Can be viewed by anyone with blockchain explorer tools

## Third-Party Services

The app communicates with:
- **Solana blockchain** (public RPC nodes) - for wallet balance and transactions
- **Your wallet app** (via MWA) - for transaction signing

Neither we nor these services collect personal data through this app.

## Children's Privacy

Battleship Sol does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated for general audiences.

## Security

While we don't collect data, we implement security best practices:
- Secure wallet communication via Mobile Wallet Adapter
- No sensitive data storage
- Open-source security audits welcome
